About Us

Our Story

ProFit is a one-of-a-kind Health & Fitness application for web and mobile, designed to help industry professionals save time, generate business, stand out in the market, stay organized, and most importantly profit!

Our mission is to help you improve quality of service and product, generate business for our clients and thus provide them with additional revenue streams. We aim to improve the flow and efficiency of your day-to-day business, and health and exercise program creation and implementation. Our end goal is having our clients enjoy the luxury of spending more of their time on what matters and what they love!
